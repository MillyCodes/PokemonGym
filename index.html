Balance · Grace · Power

Founded in 1998 by the water master Misty, Cerulean Gym has been a haven for water trainers and their Pokémon for over two decades.

Commanding her ferocious Slowpoke, Leader Milly and her assistant trainers Tom and Marky welcome all challengers who have earned their Pewter City gym badge.

About Cerulean Gym

The Cerulean Gym is the official Gym of Cerulean City. It is based on Water-type Pokémon. The Gym Leader is Milly. Trainers who defeat her will receive the Cascade Badge.
